About Me

Hi, my name is Sjur and im a programmer, teacher, geologist and glaciologist. Currently I'm a student at the University of Bergen, finishing a degree in ice sheet modelling. I like meeting new people, learning new things and I have a number of varying interest. As a hobby photographer I have learnt to develop my own film, and I have documented my field work and other travels. I also really enjoy filmmaking and editing. I often work on my own projects, coding small games and app ideas.

After a bachellor in Computer Engineering, I wanted to work, climb and travel before continuing my studies. And that's excactly what i did. In 2018 I took the bullet train around China, camped in the bushes of Eastern Australia and bouldered for months in Rocklands, South Africa. When I returned I started a five year combined masters program in geology, mathematics and teaching. My programming background gave me an advantage in several aspects of the program, and I eventually chose a thesis project with programming in focus: Numerical modelling of ice sheet movement.
